The online-platform model

Software drafts. No privilege.

Online legal platforms generate documents from questionnaire inputs. LegalZoom's own disclosure states that information provided to LegalZoom is not protected by attorney-client privilege. Their "attorney consultations" are separate 30-minute network calls — the lawyer on that call did not draft the document, does not sign it, and carries no malpractice coverage over it.

The Lozano model

Attorney-drafted. Privileged.

Every document prepared by Lozano Legal Advisors is drafted, reviewed, and taken under professional responsibility by a licensed Texas attorney. The attorney-client relationship — and the privilege that comes with it — begins from the first conversation, not after a separate network-lawyer call. The AI accelerates the drafting. The attorney does the practicing.
