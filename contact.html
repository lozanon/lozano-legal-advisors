Elder law is the legal practice devoted to the needs of older adults and their families — planning for long-term care, qualifying for public benefits like Medicaid, protecting the home and a lifetime of savings, providing for the healthy spouse who remains at home, and putting powers of attorney in place before capacity declines. At Lozano Legal Advisors, elder law and Medicaid planning is the firm’s lead practice: legal planning that helps Texas families pay for nursing home care without losing everything they built. It’s also the right place to start for an estate plan, a tax question, a real estate closing, or a business formation. Response within one business day.
